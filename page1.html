The Adventure of Whisp and Omo

CHAPTER ONE OUT NOW!
The Adventure of Whisp and Omo follows two characters on a heartfelt adventure through a fantasy settings. Traverse gorgeous hand crafted environments, and solve simple puzzles, all while progressing through the story. 
This is now my most ambitious project yet (no longer Robo Rampage).
spanning nearly 400 hours of recorded development time, and following industry practices the whole way through.
The game was made in the Unity game engine, and coded in C#. I also built all the 3D assets for the game using Blender. Each digital illustration was done in Photoshop. This project allowed me to expand my skills tremendously, both with the software involved, and mental state. 
To play the game you must download it, however it is available for both windows and mac. You may also be interested in the source files or the industry-style documentation

Naughts & Crosses meets Mini Golf

Naughts & Crosses meets Mini Golf was a project where the goal was to out a spin on a existing game, in this case i altered naughts and crosses. The game is physics based golf game where each player takes turns in completing the golf course. Instead of a hole at the end there is a 3x3 grid and each player to capture a point on the grid gains that position in the game of naughts and crosses.
Almost every aspect of the game was developed by me, including the 3D assets, game mechanics and all the levels. 
You can play the game for free in your browser as well as check out the GitHub repo.
