About Me

Hi my name is Jacob and I'm currently studying game development at East Sussex College in Lewes England. I have a passion for 3D game design and want break into the industry. I plan to study this further with an undergraduate degree at university.
I have experience with Unity, Blender, Version Control (Git), C#, C++ and many industry practices, such as design documentation, stand-ups etc. I also have plenty of soft skills like project management, presentations and social skills I also have a limited understanding off Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects, Audacity, and a few more. 
Next year i wish to study at university to specialize in 3D game design (maybe level design, maybe mechanics design).

My life isn't all game dev though I also have a few hobbies that allow me to escape the world of game creation. I enjoy walking my dog, photography, music, building computers, consuming media and of-course playing games.
